GLOBAL HUMAN TRAFFICKING REPORT (link)

       In this project, I collected global data on human trafficking. My goal was to answer the questions "HOW MANY people are victims of human trafficking across the globe among documented database cases? WHO are they? WHY are they being trafficked? And WHERE are they being trafficked?" My wife and I spent some time living in SE Asia working with human trafficking victims, becoming very involved and up to date on the current global state of human trafficking. I collected my data set from Ctdatacollaborative.org which is the single most comprehensive and accurate global dataset about human trafficking victims in the world.
